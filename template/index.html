What we do

Open to all skill levels, we share tips, work on projects together and have technical and community discussions. Bring a laptop.

Where we meet

We rotate between venues such as HSBXL and BeCentral, with pop-up sessions planned in Nivelles, Ghent and other cities.

Format

Expect short demos, lightning talks and informal hacking sessions. We typically start early evening and wrap up when the conversation winds down.
